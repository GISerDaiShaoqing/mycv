    Dec. 4, 2017: Our research determined the association between the area of submersion and the change area of landscape by data mining. Using the change in ecology indices, we ascertained the changing spatial patterns of the NGI landscape and used these findings to formulate suggestions for Sponge City planning in Fuzhou. Overall, our work combines three research themes, urban waterlogging, landscape ecology, and data mining, for a deep analysis of the relationship between the NGI landscape and storm runoff in an urban environment. This paper published on International Low Impact Development Conference China 2016 : LID Applications in Sponge City Projects
