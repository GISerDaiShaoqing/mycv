The Optimization Model of High Education Staff’s Refuge Space Under Earthquake Disater

 

Abstract

Earthquake is a devastating natural disaster, The seismic refuge has become the research focus in the world. Status quo of Qishan campus of Fujian Normal University earthquake disaster prone and vulnerability based on, The campus network model using L-A coupled seismic refuge constrained model and the evaluation method of MCE deployment optimization research. The results are as follows. 1）The principle of combining with national disaster in earthquake emergency shelter planning based on planning, 20 earthquake emergency shelter. 2）Fujian Normal University college student population spatial distribution pattern showed uneven spatial distribution, the characteristics of the North south. Under different time scales and different obvious spatial agglomeration. 3) According to the spatial and temporal distribution of proposed model assumptions, in different time scales according to different locations of the evacuation route analysis. And the crowd under earthquake disaster evacuation of about 64.412m/min by using the MCE evaluation method and entropy method. According to the evaluation method of MCE results, the use of L-A network constraint model to evaluate campus earthquake evacuation capability. The whole campus evacuation capability is weak, scenario simulation refuge accommodate the number of students is 18638, the shelter utilization rate is only 30%. The need for further planning and construction of disaster prevention and mitigation.

Key Words

L-A network model, The temporal and spatial distribution pattern of population, MCE evaluation, The University Campus
